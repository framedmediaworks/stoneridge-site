The Community

Everything right
where you need it

StoneRidge is a master-planned community nestled within the foothills and boulders of the Bradshaw Mountains, blending the greenery of a world-class golf course with hundreds of acres of open space like a hidden gallery.

Backing up to the Prescott National Forest at 5,200 feet, this desert/mountain setting delivers a mild, four-season climate you won't find in the Valley. Shopping, medical care and restaurants sit just outside the gates, and downtown Prescott is minutes away.

You're a few minutes from charming downtown Prescott, and just over an hour north of Phoenix/Scottsdale — the best of all worlds.
