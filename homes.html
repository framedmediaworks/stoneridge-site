How It Works

The Homebuying
Process

Our goal is a home buying experience that's as easy and fun as possible. You can relax — you are in StoneRidge!

01

Get Pre-Qualified

A pre-qualification letter helps you determine the floor plan and amenities that fit your budget, and your lender will walk you through loan options. StoneRidge can connect you with a preferred lender, or you may work with your own.

Preferred Lender Website
02

Select From Available Homes

Consider what lot, plan and features matter to you — garage space, kitchen features, bath layouts. When a new home is available for sale, it's posted on our Available Homes page.

Available Homes
03

Contract

Making an offer couldn't be easier — fill out the offer worksheet from the comfort of your own home and our sales team will be in touch to answer any questions. Once accepted, a purchase agreement is prepared and a non-refundable deposit is required before papers head to escrow and your lender.

Home Offer Form
04

Homeowner Orientation

You'll receive details for your Final Homeowner Orientation with a StoneRidge representative — an overview of your home and a chance to note any items needing attention before closing.

05

Appraisal & Signing

The lender requests the appraisal and begins underwriting, and arrangements are made for you to sign your closing documents at the escrow company.

06

Move In!

Congratulations — you're the proud new owner of a StoneRidge home. Once your loan is recorded, a representative will present you with the keys.

07

Warranty Period

We check in twice after move-in — at your 45-Day Care Check-up and again at your 11-Month Tune-up — to make sure everything's going well.
