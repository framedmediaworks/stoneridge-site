StoneRidge Golf Club

Eighteen holes,
350 feet of elevation

Designed by Randy Heckenkemper, this desert/mountain hybrid course climbs and falls through washes and canyons — one of the most scenic rounds in Arizona.

The 7,052-yard, par-72 course offers a scenic yet challenging experience for golfers of every skill level. Average daily highs run roughly 20 degrees cooler than the Phoenix area, making it a wonderful course to play year-round.

StoneRidge features an excellent practice facility, a fully appointed pro shop and on-site golf instruction — and Hole #18 is something you truly have to experience to believe.
