Golf

StoneRidge Golf Club

Designed by Randy Heckenkemper, this 18-hole course offers a scenic yet challenging experience for golfers of all skill levels. This desert/mountain hybrid course is one of the most beautiful courses in Arizona.

The 7,052-yard, par-72 course is dramatic, climbing and falling more than 350 feet in elevation through fairways and greens that wind among the washes and canyons. Average daily highs run roughly 20 degrees cooler than the Phoenix area.

StoneRidge offers an excellent practice facility, a fully appointed pro shop and golf instruction. Hole #18 is something you have to experience to believe.

Wellness

StoneRidge Community Center

As a resident of the StoneRidge community, you have access to a community center where you can challenge each other on our tennis and pickleball courts, cool off in our indoor and outdoor pools, and create memories at our beautiful clubhouse.

We also have a fitness room, yoga room, kitchen and a multi-purpose gathering space for a game of pool, activities, clubs and classes.

In addition to the Community Center, StoneRidge offers miles of hiking and biking trails for those who prefer getting their cardio outside. Our community is perfect for someone who wants easy access to city life while maintaining the peace and beauty of small-town living.

Dining

The Prescott Club Restaurant

Join your friends after a day of golf at The Prescott Club. The outdoor patio is open and boasts some of the prettiest views in the Prescott area.

Come enjoy a little nosh and a cocktail or an ice cold beer. Everything on the menu is wonderful. Come listen to some live music with some of the most friendly people in the area.
